My principles!

Hi, my name i Cecilia and I am a UX/UI-designer!
I am a highly motivated and solution-driven designer, ready to tackle challenges of all sizes. I thrive on turning problems into creative, solutions.
I believe that research lies in the heart of the design process, to see the users needs and wants from the very start will result in a great end product. This will pave the way for thoughtful, and impactful designs.
